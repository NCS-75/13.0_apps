Repair Service - Vendor Portal

This module allows to send a purchase quote on email to the vendor. On receiving the email the vendor can view the quotation with a click of a button available on the quotation mail. On verifying the quotation, the vendor can accept or reject it with a click of button available on the quotation. On accepting the quotation, it is verified with an e-signature and forward it with 'Accepted' button. The quotation can be rejected with a reason/comment which can be forwarded with the 'Reject' button.

This module is compatible with

  • Community
  • Enterprise
  • Odoo.sh

Key Features

    Online confirmation of purchase quotation through vendor portal.
    Vendor acceptance with e-signature functionality.
    Easy to download digitally signed quotation in PDF format.
    Vendor rejection with comments/reason.
